About

Learn More About Us

Our studio is located in the south end of Bucharest and our jewelry can be found in several high-end boutique shops throughout the city as well in several shops around the world. Our team is comprised of myself (Anastasia) and several close Romanian friends who are talented metalsmiths. Together we handmake and deliver all of our products from our studio. Since 2020 we have created more than 10,000 custom jewelry pieces. Onsite we design, mold, fire, and finish all products.

With early roots in the mountains of Brasov to later the shores of Bucharest, Anastasia' deep appreciation for culture, art and nature has manifested itself in the form of unique, one a kind jewelry. Every piece is handcrafted in Bucharest and made with Intention, Love, and Soul so that it can be felt by it's wearer and ultimately change our energy and lives in a positive way. Each with it's own story of insight and love, our pieces, like humans, are organic and asymmetrical in form.

Our collective mission is to empower and inspire others to live as their best selves while letting go of fear and breaking the boundaries of societal expectations and constraints that often imprison the soul. With odes to spiritualism, artistic displays of expressionism, and ancient cultures, our pieces have a story to tell and insight to share as you embark on your journey of freedom, adventure, and magic.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • When will my order ship?

    We are currently running about 5-7 business days processing time (handmade and/or pick from stock, pack, ship). Our handmade process involves molding, firing, and hand finishing every piece here in our Bucharest studio. We ask for your patience during this process. We are bringing on more artisans here in Bucharest at the moment and will return to our faster production times after the holidays.

  • How will I know when my order ships?

    You will receive an email when your order ships that contains the tracking number.

  • What if I need an item by a specific date?

    If you need something by a specific date, please contact us well in advance and we'll do our best to accommodate you. However, please note, we currently are shipping from Bucharest, Romania so we are limited as to how fast we can get items to you.

  • How do i track my package?

    You will receive an email when your order ships that contains the tracking number.

  • What happens if my package is deemed lost?

    We copy and paste the exact delivery address and phone number used for your order. If your package is deemed lost by the carrier, we are not responsible for undeliverable addresses. Refunds on lost packages are subject to a 20% restocking fee or we can remake the item for free 1 time and resend to a new address of your choice.

  • What countries to you ship to?

    We currently ship to all countries. Customers are responsible for any import custom fees associated with their order for their country.
